NAME:
    print speed simulator

SYNOPSIS
    ./rk1 [Difficulty level]
          [Max time]
          [Max typo count]

DESCRIPTION:          
    Welcome to the print speed simulator!
    Here you can test your printing speed. There are 3 difficulty levels: easy, medium and hard.
    How to play?
    You have to set the "Difficulty level" (required), and you can also choose 
    the maximum printing time ("Max time") and the maximum allowable number of errors ("Max typo count").
    GOOD LUCK!

OPTIONS:
    Difficulty levels:
        easy medium hard debug
    Max time:
        in sec, natural number
    Max typo count:
        natural number
